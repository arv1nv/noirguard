Our Process

Curious about how we work? Here's a quick look at our simple, three-step process to keep your driveway looking its best.

First Step: Clean & Prepare

We start by thoroughly cleaning your driveway, removing dirt, debris, and stains to ensure a strong bond.

Second Step: Repair & Fill Cracks

Next, we fill any cracks or holes to create a smooth, even surface and prevent future damage.

Final Step: Seal & Protect

Finally, we apply a high-quality sealant to protect your driveway and enhance its appearance, followed by proper drying time.
